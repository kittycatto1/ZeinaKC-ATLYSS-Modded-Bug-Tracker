# EverythingMaps Statistics
Statistical analysis of all (non-deprecated) MapLoader maps put into a spreadsheet. Note that this is reading off of the MapBundle.\
The information is gathered by checking "Properties" on MapBundles files & Asset folders reading "Size" instead of "Size on disk". "Misc" refers to other MapLoader files that aren't the MapBundle.\
"Uncompressed" is the actual* size of the map before being bundled (compressed), the information was obtained using [AssetRipper](https://github.com/AssetRipper/AssetRipper) by exportng primary content.
